How to use the Client Hub

Learn how to use the powerful features available to you.

Navigation & Basics

Switching Channels: In the 'Chats' tab, simply click on any channel name in the list to view its messages.

Creating a Channel: Click the icon at the top of the sidebar to create a new client group.

Attaching a File: Click the icon in the message input bar to open your file browser and select a file to share.

Using Sidebar Tabs: Click the 'Chats', 'Files', 'Activity', or 'Automate' icons to switch between different views and features in the sidebar.

Video Calls & Transcription

Starting a Call: Click the icon in the chat header to open the meeting lobby. Share the link and click "Join Now".

Recording & Transcribing: Once in a call, click the Record button. After you stop the recording, the audio will be automatically transcribed by AI. A notification will appear in the chat.

Viewing a Transcript: Click the 'View Transcript' button in the notification, or click the icon in the chat header to open and close the transcription panel on the right.

Creating How-To Guides

Start Recording: Click the icon in the chat header. This will start a screen share session.

Capture Steps: As you perform actions on your screen, click the "Capture Step" button in the control bar at the bottom. Each click saves a screenshot.

Generate Guide: When you're done, click "Finish & Generate". Our AI will analyze each screenshot, write a description for the action, and post a complete step-by-step guide directly into the chat.
