How to use the Client Hub

Learn how to use the powerful features available to you.

Video Calls & Transcription

Starting a Call: Click the icon in the chat header to open the meeting lobby. Share the link and click "Join Now".

Recording & Transcribing: Once in a call, click the Record button. After you stop the recording, the audio will be automatically transcribed by AI. A notification will appear in the chat, and you can view the full transcript in the side panel.

Creating How-To Guides

Start Recording: Click the icon in the chat header. This will start a screen share session.

Capture Steps: As you perform actions on your screen, click the "Capture Step" button in the control bar at the bottom. Each click saves a screenshot.

Generate Guide: When you're done, click "Finish & Generate". Our AI will analyze each screenshot, write a description for the action, and post a complete step-by-step guide directly into the chat.

Chat & Channels

Switching Channels: Simply click on any channel name in the left-hand list to view its messages.

Creating a Channel: Click the icon at the top of the channel list to create a new client group or discussion space.
